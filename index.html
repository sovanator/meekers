Kia Ora! Welcome!

Our program consists of two parts: Be a host family for an exchange student or one of their adult teachers & chaperones from New Zealand. Our New Zealand friends travel to the US in September each year. They stay with their host families approximately 2 1/2 weeks. and/or Travel to New Zealand as an Ambassador! Teachers apply in February-March each year. Students & chaperones are then selected in April-May each year. From May to February, the group meets regularly to fundraise & prepare for their travels in February.
